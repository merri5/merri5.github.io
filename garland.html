x DIY: Glitter Bow Garland x




Get a head start on holiday decorating with today's sparkly DIY!



Supplies

Bow tie pasta
Metallic spray paint
Glitter
Decorative baker's twine


All you need to do to create this simple and adorable holiday garland is spray paint the pasta, sprinkle with glitter and tie on the twine.



Now you're ready to add some festive flair to your tree or mantel!
